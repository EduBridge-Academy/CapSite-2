Frequently Asked Questions

What is the goal of the Career Acceleration Program?

It is an online work experience program. You will have the opportunity to learn key work skills, work in a team, complete work projects and earn a certificate to prove your achievements. It is targeted towards students in final or penultimate year, corps members, graduates expecting NYSC call up and job seekers. In general people with zero to 2 years work experience are a good fit

Would I intern with Edubridge or a third party employer?

This virtual program is with EduBridge Academy for the purpose of your learning and workplace development. You would learn with Edubridge and intern with third party employers to gain experience. The projects that you will work on are of benefit to your learning and development hence you will not receive payment for the activities.

Would employers be waiting for me at the end of the program?

EduBridge Academy does not promise to match you to employers at the end of this program. However most participants get placements during and after the program.

What are the benefits to me?

Mentorship, Work experience, Professional community, workplace skills, improve chances of getting a job are some of the benefits you would obtain by participating in this program.

Can I make payments installmentally?

Yes, you can pay in installments but you have to pay your first installments before the program begins.

Can I combine this with my job?

Yes you can, the program is intense but you can pace yourself as you must complete all tasks and activities.
